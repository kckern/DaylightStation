Design: Cast Action Flow

Section 3 of 4 — What happens when you tap cast on content

Cast Button Behavior

Today's CastButton opens DevicePicker and immediately fires. New behavior depends on whether a target is set.

No Target Set → Old Flow
Tap 📡 on content:
1. Opens DevicePicker modal
↓
2. Pick device → sets it as target
↓
3. Shows per-cast options (below)
↓
4. Fires to device

Falls back to the old modal flow, but now the device sticks as your target for subsequent casts.

Target Active → Fast Path
Tap 📡 on content:
1. Inline popover with per-cast options
↓
2. Tap "Cast" → fires to target device

Skip device selection entirely. Device settings (shader, volume) already configured via header. Just confirm per-cast options and go.

Per-Cast Popover

Small popover anchored to the cast button. Shows only playback-level options — the device stuff is already handled.
