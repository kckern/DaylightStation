  Former Peloton instructor Jennifer Jacobs brings her signature performance-driven coaching to 20-minute cycling workouts built around four pillars: endurance, speed, strength, and power. Each ride has a purpose — In the Zone for steady-state work, HIIT Your Peak for interval bursts, Embrace the Climb for hill strength, Fast 20 for speed, and Power Tempo for sustained output. Three weeks of progressive riding will break through your ceiling and make you fitter both on and off the bike.
